ABOUT THE TEAM

The textile industry is primarily concerned with the design, production and distribution of textiles: yarn, cloth and clothing. The raw material may be natural, or synthetic using products of the chemical industry. These workers are called opener tenders, picker tenders, card tenders, drawing frame tenders, and roving tenders. Frame spinners operate machines that spin the fiber into yarn.
